Ana Daniela Lara

UX/UI Front-end Developer

Hi! I’m a UX/UI designer and front-end developer with a creative and user-centered mindset. I love designing websites and interfaces that not only look beautiful but also feel intuitive and easy to use.

I’m especially passionate about web design, personal branding, and building experiences that connect with people. I work with tools like Figma, Adobe XD, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React — and I really enjoy turning ideas into interactive designs that come to life on screen.

What drew me into this world is my love for creativity and the feeling of seeing my designs interact with real users. I find a lot of inspiration in art, architecture, and anything visual. I'm very detail-oriented and I enjoy solving problems through thoughtful design.

If there’s one thing I hope people remember about me, it’s that I put a lot of heart into everything I create. I'm dedicated, passionate, and always excited to keep learning and growing through each new project.
